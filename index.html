Introducing JUTICA

The Gold Standard of Eco-Luxury

Product Identity

JUTICA is a premium, waterproof jute tote engineered for the conscious urban demographic. It uses high-density 300 GSM golden jute fiber to ensure a rugged, natural look without the fragility of synthetic alternatives.

Financial Vitality

  • Retail Price: ₹250.00
  • Target Cost: ₹180.00
  • Actual Cost: ₹174.70
  • Margin: 28%

Project Overview

This project explores the strategic application of Target Costing to launch "JUTICA" — a premium, eco-friendly jute tote bag. Our goal is to achieve a sustainable competitive advantage by aligning product design with market-driven pricing.

Primary Objective

To plan and control costs effectively by deriving the Target Cost from a competitive Selling Price and Desired Profit, rather than the traditional cost-plus approach.

Key Methodologies

  • Market Research: To validate demand & price point.
  • Value Engineering: To optimize material costs.
  • Kaizen Costing: To ensure continuous improvement in production.

Continuous Improvement (Kaizen)

Strategic Cost Management

Two strategic techniques deployed to minimize deviation and optimize cost:

Technique 1: Kaizen Costing

Implementing small, continuous improvements in the labor process. Daily stand-ups identify assembly bottlenecks, reducing time per unit and labor costs.

Technique 2: Value Engineering

Analyzed material functions vs. cost. Replaced heavy brass fittings with durable recycled composite (lower cost, same "Eco-Luxury" function).
